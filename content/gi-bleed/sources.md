# Essential Sources & Guidelines for GI Bleed Management

1.  **American College of Gastroenterology (ACG) - Management of Patients With Ulcer Bleeding (2021)**
    * *Focus:* Non-variceal upper GI bleeding, PPI dosing, and endoscopic timing.
    * *Link:* [https://journals.lww.com/ajg/Fulltext/2021/05000/ACG_Clinical_Guideline__Upper_Gastrointestinal_and.14.aspx](https://journals.lww.com/ajg/Fulltext/2021/05000/ACG_Clinical_Guideline__Upper_Gastrointestinal_and.14.aspx)

2.  **American Association for the Study of Liver Diseases (AASLD) - Portal Hypertensive Bleeding in Cirrhosis (2016/Updated)**
    * *Focus:* Variceal screening, primary prophylaxis, and acute variceal hemorrhage management (Octreotide, Ceftriaxone, TIPS).
    * *Link:* [https://aasldpubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/hep.28906](https://aasldpubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/hep.28906)

3.  **American College of Gastroenterology (ACG) - Management of Patients With Acute Lower Gastrointestinal Bleeding (2016)**
    * *Focus:* Resuscitation, risk assessment, colonoscopy timing, and role of CTA/IR in lower GI bleeds.
    * *Link:* [https://journals.lww.com/ajg/Fulltext/2016/04000/Management_of_Patients_With_Acute_Lower.14.aspx](https://journals.lww.com/ajg/Fulltext/2016/04000/Management_of_Patients_With_Acute_Lower.14.aspx)

4.  **Internet Book of Critical Care (IBCC) - Upper GI Bleed**
    * *Focus:* High-yield, pragmantic ICU perspective on resuscitation, intubation decisions, and medication management.
    * *Link:* [https://emcrit.org/ibcc/ugib/](https://emcrit.org/ibcc/ugib/)

5.  **New England Journal of Medicine - Transfusion Strategies for Acute Upper Gastrointestinal Bleeding (Villanueva et al.)**
    * *Focus:* Landmark trial supporting restrictive (Hb < 7) vs liberal (Hb < 9) transfusion thresholds. 
    * *Link:* [https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa1211801](https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa1211801)
