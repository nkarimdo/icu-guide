# AKI Sources & Reference Material

## Guidelines & Literature
* [KDIGO Clinical Practice Guideline for Acute Kidney Injury](https://kdigo.org/guidelines/acute-kidney-injury/): The gold-standard international framework for defining and managing AKI.
* [American Society of Nephrology (ASN) - AKI Resources](https://www.asn-online.org/education/distancelearning/curricula/aki/): Excellent curriculum modules for clinical review.

## Clinical Tools
* [MDCalc: Fractional Excretion of Sodium (FENa)](https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/39/fractional-excretion-sodium-fena): Quick bedside calculator.
* [MDCalc: Fractional Excretion of Urea (FEUrea)](https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/40/fractional-excretion-urea-feurea): Essential for oliguric patients currently on active diuresis.

## Point-of-Care Reference
* [UpToDate: Evaluation of acute kidney injury among hospitalized adult patients](https://www.uptodate.com/contents/evaluation-of-acute-kidney-injury-among-hospitalized-adult-patients): A comprehensive overview of the diagnostic pathway.
* [UpToDate: Overview of the management of acute kidney injury (AKI) in adults](https://www.uptodate.com/contents/overview-of-the-management-of-acute-kidney-injury-aki-in-adults): Therapeutics and indications for renal replacement therapy.
