# Sources & References for DKA Management

The protocols for Diabetic Ketoacidosis are heavily based on guidelines published by major diabetes and endocrinology associations. 

1. **American Diabetes Association (ADA) - Standards of Care in Diabetes**
   * *Description:* The definitive, annually updated guide for all diabetes care, including diabetic emergencies.
   * *Link:* [ADA Standards of Care in Diabetes](https://diabetesjournals.org/care/issue/47/Supplement_1)

2. **American Association of Clinical Endocrinology (AACE)**
   * *Description:* Clinical practice guidelines and algorithms for comprehensive diabetes care and emergency management.
   * *Link:* [AACE Guidelines](https://pro.aace.com/disease-state-resources/diabetes)

3. **BMJ Best Practice: Diabetic Ketoacidosis**
   * *Description:* Step-by-step clinical guidance on diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up.
   * *Link:* [BMJ Best Practice - DKA](https://bestpractice.bmj.com/topics/en-us/119)

4. **StatPearls: Diabetic Ketoacidosis**
   * *Description:* A comprehensive, peer-reviewed clinical summary of DKA pathophysiology, evaluation, and treatment.
   * *Link:* [NCBI StatPearls - DKA](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK534127/)
